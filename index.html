About The Fest
What Is VidhYouth?

VidhYouth is the annual technical and cultural extravaganza of the Electrical Engineering Department, Ujjain Engineering College.

Three electrifying days packed with competitions, expert talks, performances, and pure energy — where curious minds collide with creative souls.

Whether you're a tech wizard, a cultural maverick, or just here to feel the surge — VidhYouth has something that'll spark you.
