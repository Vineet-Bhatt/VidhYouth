General E-Sports Rules
Fair Play First
📱
Device Policy
Players must use their own mobile devices. Rooted phones, emulators, tablets and third-party controllers are strictly not allowed. Devices may be checked by organizers at any time.
🪪
Eligibility
Open to all students of Ujjain Engineering College with valid student ID. All team members must be currently enrolled students. One player cannot be part of two teams across the same game.
⏰
Reporting Time
All players must report at the venue at least 15 minutes before their scheduled match. Failure to report on time may result in a walkover for the opposing team.
🚫
Zero Tolerance
Hacks, mods, aimbots, wall hacks, or any form of cheating results in permanent disqualification from all E-Sports events. Verbal abuse or unsportsmanlike conduct will also result in DQ.
⚖️
Disputes
All decisions made by the tournament organizer are final. Disputes must be raised within 10 minutes of match end. Screenshots/recordings may be submitted as evidence.
